# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title

**📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard**

A dynamic and interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed to analyze retail sales performance across products, customers, stores, and regions. The dashboard provides actionable business insights through executive KPIs, sales trend analysis, regional comparisons, customer analytics, and interactive filtering capabilities.

---

# 2. Purpose

The **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to help business users monitor and analyze retail sales performance using transactional data. It enables users to explore sales trends, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, regional sales distribution, and payment preferences through dynamic visualizations and interactive slicers.

This dashboard is intended for business managers, sales analysts, retail executives, and decision-makers who require a centralized reporting solution to monitor KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

# 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main Business Intelligence platform used for designing interactive reports and dashboards.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for importing, cleaning, transforming, and preparing raw retail datasets before analysis.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Implemented dynamic measures and business calculations such as Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers, Profit Margin, and Average Order Value.
* 📝 **Data Modeling** – Built a Star Schema by establishing relationships between Sales, Customers, Products, Stores, and Date tables for efficient filtering and reporting.
* 📅 **Date Table** – Created using DAX to enable time-based analysis including Year, Month, Quarter, and Week reporting.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for dashboard development, `.xlsx` for datasets, and `.png` for dashboard previews.

---

# 4. Data Source

**Source:** Sample Retail Sales Dataset (Excel Files)

The project is built using a multi-table retail sales dataset containing transactional and master data across different business entities.

The dataset consists of four primary tables:

* Sales
* Customers
* Products
* Stores

A dedicated **Date Table** was created within Power BI using DAX to support time intelligence and chronological reporting.

The data includes information such as customer purchases, product details, sales amount, profit, discounts, payment modes, store information, and order dates, enabling comprehensive retail sales analysis from multiple business perspectives.

# 5. Features

## 📌 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every day. However, raw transactional data alone makes it difficult to identify performance trends, monitor business KPIs, and make strategic decisions.

Business stakeholders often need answers to questions such as:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which product categories contribute the most profit?
* How many customers placed orders?
* Which payment method is most preferred?
* How are monthly sales performing?
* Which store types generate better business performance?

Without an interactive reporting solution, answering these questions requires manual analysis and significant time.

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The primary goal of this dashboard is to provide an interactive Business Intelligence solution that transforms raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard enables users to:

* Monitor business performance through executive KPIs.
* Analyze monthly sales trends.
* Compare regional sales performance.
* Evaluate product category profitability.
* Analyze store type contribution.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Explore payment mode distribution.
* Filter reports dynamically using interactive slicers.
